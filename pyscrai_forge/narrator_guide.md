## Overview
The Narrative Generator creates data-driven narratives from your project entities with AI-powered fact-checking. It transforms raw project data into coherent, engaging narratives while ensuring accuracy.

## How to Use

### 1. Generation Settings

**Narrative Mode**
- **auto**: AI selects the best format based on your data
- **sitrep**: Situation report format (current status, analysis)
- **story**: Narrative storytelling style
- **dossier**: Detailed intelligence briefing
- **summary**: Concise overview

**Entity Filter**
- **all**: Include all project entities
- **actor**: Focus on individuals and organizations
- **polity**: Focus on political entities and nations
- **location**: Focus on geographical locations

**Focus** (optional)
Enter specific topics, themes, or questions to guide the narrative generation.

4. Refine for accuracy and coherence

Generation typically takes 30-60 seconds depending on project size.

## Tips
- Use **auto mode** for first-time generation
- Add a **focus** to target specific aspects of your project
- **Entity filters** help create specialized reports
- Generated narratives cite source data and maintain factual accuracy
